Welcome to the Nier Automata fanwiki page!

This site is dedicated to fans of the Nier Automata video game who want to delve deeper into its world, characters, and story.

Nier Automata is an action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix in 2017.

The game takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where human-like androids fight against machine invaders to reclaim Earth for their creators.

The game's intricate narrative, immersive gameplay, and memorable characters have earned it critical acclaim and a devoted fanbase.

Whether you're a veteran player or a newcomer to the game, this wiki is here to help you explore and understand everything that Nier Automata has to offer.

Note: Be wary of unmarked spoilers! Read at your own risk.
